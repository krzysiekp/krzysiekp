Extended Fraud Protection

Create a first line of defence against fraudalent orders.

Download .zip Download .tar.gz View on GitHub

Overview

Fraudsters attack multiple services provider at once - why not work together to prevent this from happening. Join other HostBill users in fight against fraud.

  • lowering fraud risk - use data gathered by HostBill to distinguish potential fraud orders automatically.
  • using free geolocation databases to protect from order country IP missmatch.
  • reduce your Maxmind and other fraud protection fees.

Supported Features

  • Remote IP check against HostBill fraud protection database.
  • Option to reject orders from IPs related with fraud (based on local data).
  • You can use local geolocation database to reject country missmatch.
  • Automatically marking as fraud orders from defined countries.
  • Optional verification for returning customers.
  • Orders submitted by staff members are not verified.

Installation & Updates

Only requirement for this plug-in is working HostBill installation. Installation can be done in three steps:

  • Download Extended Fraud Protection from your client area
  • Upload & unzip it in your HostBill root directory
  • Activate it in your module manager in HostBIll

To update to newer version you will just have to repeat step 1 & 2.

What is IPAM?.

IPAM (IP Address Management) is designed to organize and track IP addresses in your organization/company/department. Its key features consist of:

  • IPv4 & IPv6 management.
  • Sub-net management options.
  • Integration with DNS services to provide easy reverse DNS management.
  • Import options from most common hosting software like OnApp, cPanel and more.

Administration area integration will let you quickly check which ips were assigned to your client, you will also have new options for dedicated servers and colocation that allow assigning of IPs directly to accounts.

Installation & Updates

Only requirement for this plug-in is working HostBill installation. Installation can be done in three steps: 1. Download IPAM package from your client area 1. Upload & unzip it in your HostBill root directory 1. Activate it in your module manager in HostBIll 1. asd

To update to newer version you will just have to repeat step 1 & 2.
